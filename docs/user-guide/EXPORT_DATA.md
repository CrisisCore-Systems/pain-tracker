# How to Export Your Data

Pain Tracker gives you full control over your data. You can export your pain entries, analytics, and reports in multiple formats at any time.

---

## Export Formats

| Format | Best For | Contents |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **PDF** | Sharing with doctors, printing | Formatted clinical report with charts and summaries |
| **CSV** | Spreadsheets, further analysis | Raw data in comma-separated format |
| **JSON** | Backup, data portability | Complete structured data export |

---

## How to Export

### Standard Export

1. Open Pain Tracker and unlock your vault
2. Navigate to the **Export** section (available from the dashboard or settings)
3. Select your export format (PDF, CSV, or JSON)
4. Choose a date range (or export all entries)
5. Click **Export** / **Download**
6. The file downloads to your device

### WorkSafeBC Clinical Report (PDF)

For WorkSafeBC claims documentation:

1. Navigate to the **Export** section
2. Select **WorkSafeBC PDF Report**
3. Enter claimant information (name, claim number) if desired
4. Select the date range relevant to your claim
5. Click **Generate Report**
6. Review the generated PDF, which includes:
   - Executive summary with key pain metrics
   - Pain trend analysis
   - Functional impact assessment
   - Work impact documentation
   - Clinical recommendations based on patterns

### Quick CSV Export

For a quick data dump for spreadsheet analysis:

1. Navigate to **Export** → **CSV**
2. Select date range
3. Download the file
4. Open in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application

---

## What's Included in Exports

### PDF Clinical Report

- Patient/claimant information (if entered)
- Date range and entry count
- Pain level trends and averages
- Body location frequency
- Symptom patterns
- Medication records
- Functional impact summary
- Professional formatting suitable for clinical use

### CSV Export

- One row per pain entry
- Columns: date, time, pain level, location, quality, symptoms, medications, mood, sleep, notes
- Compatible with any spreadsheet application

### JSON Export

- Complete structured data
- Includes all entry fields and metadata
- Suitable for data backup or programmatic processing

---

## Privacy During Export

- **Exports are generated locally** — your data does not leave your device during the export process
- **Files are saved to your device** — you control where the exported file goes
- **No upload** — exported files are not sent anywhere automatically
- **You choose what to share** — export files are yours to keep private or share as you see fit

---

## Tips

- **Regular backups:** Export a JSON backup periodically to safeguard against device loss
- **Before appointments:** Generate a PDF report covering the period since your last visit
- **WorkSafeBC claims:** Use the dedicated WorkSafeBC template for consistent documentation
- **Date ranges:** Use focused date ranges to keep reports concise and relevant

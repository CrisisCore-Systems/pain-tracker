## Recommended Send Order

### First Email

Send:

- Initial outreach email.
- One-page overview PDF.
- Privacy sheet PDF.

Include as optional links rather than heavy attachments if deliverability is a concern:

- Sample worker report PDF.
- Three screenshot demo PDF.
- 30 day pilot proposal PDF.

Reason: the first message should be easy to read, low-pressure, and credible. The overview and privacy sheet answer the two most likely initial questions: what is it, and what happens to patient data?

### After A Reply

Offer:

- Sample worker report PDF.
- Three screenshot demo PDF.
- 30 day pilot proposal PDF.

Reason: these documents are more useful once someone has shown interest or asked how the tool would work in practice.

### If Contact Is Unclear

Use:

- Contact page message.
- Phone script.

Goal: identify the correct clinical, intake, occupational health, or program coordination contact before sending the full package.

### Follow-Up Timing

- First follow-up: 5 business days after the initial email.
- Second follow-up: 12 business days after the initial email.
- Final close-out: after the second follow-up if there is no response.

Keep follow-ups short and give the recipient an easy path to redirect, decline, or ask for the materials again.

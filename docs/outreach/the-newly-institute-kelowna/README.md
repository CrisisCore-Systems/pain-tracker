## Copy Boundaries Used

- PainTracker.ca is presented as a patient support and documentation tool, not a clinical system replacement.
- The copy does not imply endorsement, integration, approval, partnership, certification, or current agreement by The Newly Institute, WorkSafeBC, or any clinical program.
- The copy does not claim clinical outcomes, diagnostic capability, treatment effect, institutional adoption, fake pilots, fake metrics, or testimonials.
- Privacy and offline claims are limited to repository-supported public claims: browser-based local storage, no account required for core tracking, offline-capable use, and user-controlled export or sharing.
- WorkSafeBC is mentioned only as context for injured worker documentation, not as an endorsement or integration.

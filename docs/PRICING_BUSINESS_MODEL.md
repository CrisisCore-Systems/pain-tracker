## üéØ Business Model Overview

Pain Tracker employs a **Freemium SaaS model** with a strong emphasis on social impact and accessibility. Our core philosophy: **pain tracking should never have a paywall for patients**.

### Core Principles

1. **Free Forever for Patients** - No subscriptions, no ads, no paywalls
2. **Monetize Clinical Value** - Healthcare providers pay for enhanced features
3. **Enterprise Scale** - Health systems and insurers fund advanced integrations
4. **Open Source Foundation** - Community-driven development with commercial extensions

## üõ°Ô∏è Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Price Sensitivity Risks

**Risk:** Clinical tier too expensive for small clinics

**Mitigation:**
- Free 90-day trial for BC clinics
- Sliding scale pricing for community health centers
- Grant funding assistance (help clinics apply for funding)
- ROI calculator showing time savings vs. manual forms

---

### Competitor Price War

**Risk:** ManageMyPain drops to $0 or adds WorkSafeBC

**Mitigation:**
- Privacy-first positioning (local data vs. cloud)
- Trauma-informed UX differentiation
- Open-source community lock-in
- First-mover advantage in BC market

---

### Enterprise Sales Cycle

**Risk:** 12-18 month enterprise sales cycles delay revenue

**Mitigation:**
- Focus on clinical tier for near-term revenue
- Pilot programs with health authorities (3-6 months)
- WorkSafeBC partnership validation
- Case studies from beta customers
